THE UNTAMED

DESCRIPTION 

The Untamed (Chen Qing Ling) is a hugely 
popular 2019 Chinese fantasy drama (C-drama) following
two soulmate cultivators, Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji,
as they reunite 16 years after a tragic event to solve 
mysteries, blending xianxia (fantasy martial arts)
with intense character relationships, particularly 
their deep, subtextual bond, adapted from the web 
novel Mo Dao Zu Shi, and became a global hit on
 platforms like Netflix, Viki, and MX Player. 
Key Aspects:
Genre: Xianxia, Fantasy, Supernatural, Wuxia.
Plot: Wei Wuxian, a mischievous but talented cultivator,
is brought back to life 16 years after his death and teams
up with his stoic, righteous friend Lan Wangji to uncover 
dark conspiracies and a tragic past.
Characters: Centers on the strong, almost romantic, connection 
between the flamboyant Wei Wuxian (Xiao Zhan) and the reserved
Lan Wangji (Wang Yibo).
Origin: Based on the popular Chinese danmei (boys' love) novel
Mo Dao Zu Shi by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu, adapted to feature a strong
subtextual queer romance due to censorship.
Themes: Deals with loyalty, betrayal, greed, power struggles,
justice, and breaking societal norms.
Impact: A massive international success, praised for its character
development, world-building, and emotional depth, despite
(or because of) its low budget and censorship constraints.
